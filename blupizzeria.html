The story

From pop-up to pilgrimage

BLU started in 2021 as a pop-up on Dubai's food circuit, founded by Emirati entrepreneur Abdelrahman Alteneiji. The first real home came at the Souk at Umm Suqeim Fishing Harbour: a small terrace on the water, a wood-fired oven, and a menu that refuses to grow past seven.

The queues came, and stayed. Then a second home in Al Khawaneej, and now the oven is travelling: Abu Dhabi and beyond.
