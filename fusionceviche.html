The story

It started in an apartment kitchen in JLT

Penelope Diaz, a self-taught Peruvian chef, began by serving ceviche to her neighbours from her own apartment. The supper clubs grew into a catering business, and in 2022 into this cevichería: a small dining room where she still greets guests and asks how much spice you can take.

Key ingredients are imported from Peru, and the menu moves with what lands that week. It earned the restaurant a place on the World's 50 Best Discovery list.
